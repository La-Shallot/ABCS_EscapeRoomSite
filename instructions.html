ABCS Escape Room

Greetings Traveler, Welcome to the AB CS Escape Rooms!

A hacker has taken control of Charlotte's computer, and they locked all her pokemon game data behind a series of puzzles!

＼(〇_ｏ)／

Please solve those computer science puzzles for her, so she can regain her pokemon game progress and accomplish her dream of catching all the pokemons.

There are two paths through these escape rooms, a junior and a senior track. The junior track is recommended for grades 3 - 6, and the senior track is recommended for grades 7 - 12. For each room, watch the video to solve the corresponding puzzle, and append your track letter(J or S) to the beginning of your code. No spaces. Fill in the code on the main page, and press enter to receive instructions for the next room.

The code to enter the first room is JHelloWorld for junior track and SHelloWorld for senior track.

Good luck. ╰(▔∀▔)╯
